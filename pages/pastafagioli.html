Pasta Fagioli

Description

Pasta Fagioli, translated as "bean pasta", is a hearty and comforting pasta that can be made with staple kithcen ingredients and cannellini beans

Prep time: 25-30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of cannellini beans
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 2-3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 a yellow onion, diced
  • 2-3 stalks of celercy, diced
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/3 cup freshly grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pound of any short noodle, but in this recipe we will use rigatoni

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, add olive oil and set to medium heat
  2. Dice onion and celery into similar sized small chunks. Once diced, add to oil in sauce pan.
  3. Season veggies and oil with salt to taste
  4. After a few minutes of frying the veggies, add can of tomatoes to sauce pan
  5. Cook down the tomatoes for about 15 minutes, and then add the can of cannellini beans
  6. In a seperate medium large pot, set water to boil. Add a few pinches of salt to boiling water
  7. Once water is to a rolling boil, add dry rigatoni
  8. Once rigatoni is AL DENTE, drain boiling water. Save one cup of the pasta water before draining it all.
  9. Add al dente rigatoni to the sauce pan with the beans, and stir everything together to incorporate into a sauce, adding grated parigiano and pasta water to create a thicker consistency.
  10. Once the desired consistency is acheived, plate pasta with freshly grated parmigiano and fresch cut parsley on top
