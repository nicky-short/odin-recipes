Spanish Tortilla

Description

Despite its name, TORTILLA in Spain usually refers to this delicions and filling potato omelette

Prep time: approx. 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 2 Russet potatoes, sliced
  • 1 yellow cooking onion, sliced
  • 2-3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. After peeling the potato and onion, slice each into equal sized slivers
  2. In a medium sauce pan or skillet, add olive oil and then add the sliced potato and onion, season with salt
  3. Cook potato and onion until they are slightly translucent, and are becoming soft to the touch
  4. Remove potato and onion from the heat, and pour the mixture into a large bowl
  5. Crack and beat the 4 eggs in a small bowl
  6. After a few minutes, when the potato and onion mixture has cooled down some, pour in the raw scrambled eggs and mix everything together
  7. Once mixed in the large bowl, let everything sit, covered, for about 30 minutes. This will help the flavours meld.
  8. After 30 minutes, start by heating a frying pan and add 2 tbsp. olive oil
  9. When oil has heated, add egg mixture to the pan and let it cook
  10. After some minutes, when the eggs have cooked on one side, use a plate to carefully flip the tortilla on to the opposite side, so that you can fry the top
  11. Once both sides have been cooked, the tortilla is ready to be plated and served
